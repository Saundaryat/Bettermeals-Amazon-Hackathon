# SuperMeals - Personalized Meal Planning App

## 🍽️ What We're Building

SuperMeals is a science-backed, personalized meal planning application that transforms your health journey from biomarkers to your plate. The app provides a complete end-to-end experience for users to discover, customize, and order personalized meal plans tailored to their unique health profile.

### Core Value Proposition
**"Your Food, Your Formula"** - A science-backed meal journey personalized from your biomarkers to your plate.

## 🎯 Key Features

### 1. **Personalized Meal Planning**
- **Biomarker-Based Recommendations**: Meal plans generated from individual health data and biomarkers
- **Household Customization**: Support for multiple household members with personalized add-ons
- **Weekly Meal Rotation**: Smart meal variety with balanced nutrition across breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- **Dietary Preferences**: Accommodates various dietary restrictions and preferences

### 2. **Interactive Meal Preview**
- **Visual Meal Carousel**: Browse through daily meal plans with beautiful food imagery
- **Nutritional Information**: Detailed breakdown of meals and their health benefits
- **Personalized Add-ons**: Individual supplements and extras based on health needs
- **Meal Explanations**: Science-backed reasoning for meal selections

### 3. **Smart Grocery Management**
- **Categorized Shopping Lists**: Organized by Produce, Grains, Protein, Pantry, and Supplements
- **Pantry Integration**: Uncheck items you already have to optimize your order
- **Smart Swaps**: Intelligent ingredient substitutions and quantity recommendations
- **Cost Estimation**: Real-time pricing and budget tracking

### 4. **Seamless Checkout Experience**
- **Quick Payment Flow**: Streamlined 3-minute checkout process
- **UPI Integration**: Native Indian payment method support
- **Delivery Scheduling**: Curated delivery windows based on location
- **Order Tracking**: Real-time status updates and delivery notifications

### 5. **Health Journey Celebration**
- **Progress Tracking**: Visual progress indicators throughout the journey
- **Motivational Elements**: Inspirational quotes and celebration animations
- **Consistency Rewards**: Recognition for maintaining healthy habits


## 🚀 User Journey Flow

### 1. **Entry Point** (`/`)
- Welcome screen with app value proposition
- Clear call-to-action to view personalized meal plan
- Household ID-based routing for personalized experiences

### 2. **Introduction** (`/:householdId`)
- Personalized welcome based on household data
- Motivational messaging about health transformation
- Direct navigation to meal planning

### 3. **Meal Plan Preview** (`/meal-plan`)
- Interactive carousel of weekly meal plans
- Daily breakdown with breakfast, lunch, and dinner
- Individual add-ons and supplements per household member
- Progress tracking with step indicators

### 4. **Grocery List Management** (`/grocery-list`)
- Categorized shopping list with smart recommendations
- Pantry integration to exclude existing items
- Cost estimation and optimization suggestions
- Bulk actions for efficient list management

### 5. **Payment Checkout** (`/checkout`)
- Streamlined payment flow with UPI integration
- Delivery window selection and address confirmation
- Timer-based urgency for quick completion
- Order summary and confirmation

### 6. **Success Celebration** (`/success`)
- Animated celebration with confetti effects
- Motivational quotes and progress recognition
- Order tracking and next steps
- Options to adjust plans or track delivery

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

### Visual Identity
- **Dark Theme**: Sophisticated dark interface with glassmorphism effects
- **Gradient Accents**: Lovable-inspired color gradients for visual appeal
- **Micro-interactions**: Smooth transitions and hover effects
- **Accessibility**: WCAG compliant with proper contrast and keyboard navigation

### User Experience
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Information revealed at the right moment
- **Reduced Cognitive Load**: Clear visual hierarchy and minimal distractions
- **Mobile-First**: Optimized for touch interactions and mobile usage
- **Performance**: Fast loading times and smooth animations
